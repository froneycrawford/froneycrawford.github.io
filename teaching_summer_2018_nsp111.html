Welcome

    This course is a one-credit F&M NSP course being taught at the Arcadia University's London Center by Froney Crawford. The dates for the course in London are Sun Jul 7 through Fri Aug 10, 2018. There are also two pre-departure class meetings at F&M and two assignments that are due before departure. Most of the logistics involving travel, housing, etc. will be handled by the study abroad office in the Joseph International Center at F&M.

    The course syllabus and schedule has a lot of the academic details, including course meetings, field trips, and assignments/grading.

    The required textbook for the course is Energy, Environment, and Climate (3nd edition) by Richard Wolfson.
