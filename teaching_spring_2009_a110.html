Welcome
    Welcome to the Spring 2009 edition of Astronomy 110: Introduction to the Solar System taught by Froney Crawford. This web page will serve as the source of information, announcements, and downloads for this course. Please check it regularly.

    The required textbook for the course is The Cosmic Perspective (5th edition) by Bennett et al. The associated web site for the text (you can register here) is www.masteringastronomy.com.

    Labs for the course will be taught by Scott Lacey on Wed nights. Details are in the lab manual and will be discussed in class and lab.

    Course announcements are located at the bottom of the page.
