Welcome
    Welcome to the Fall 2011 edition of Astronomy 170: Survey of Astronomy (Section B) taught by Froney Crawford. This web page will serve as the source of information, announcements, and downloads for this course. Please check it regularly.

    The required textbook for the course is The Cosmic Perspective (6th edition) by Bennett et al. The associated web site for the text (you can register here) is www.masteringastronomy.com.

    Labs for the course will be taught by Andrea Lommen (Mon nights), Froney Crawford (Tue nights), and Beth Praton (Wed nights). Details are in the lab manual and will be discussed in class and lab. There is also a web page for the Wednesday section of the Astronomy 170 Laboratory.
