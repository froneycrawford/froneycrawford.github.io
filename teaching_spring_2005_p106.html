Welcome
    Welcome to the Spring 2005 edition of Physics 106: Fundamental Physics II taught by Froney Crawford. This web page will serve as the source of information, announcements, and downloads for the class. Please check it regularly.

    The required textbook for the course is Physics With Modern Physics for Scientists and Engineers (3rd edition), by Wolfson & Pasachoff.
