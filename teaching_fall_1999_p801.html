Froney's Physics 8.01 Recitation Page

General Homework Information and Policy

  • Late homework is not accepted without a good reason.
  • Be sure to hand homework into the appropriate recitation box before the time it is due.

General Quiz Information and Policy

  • Every quiz is on a Wednesday.
  • The quiz material is drawn from homework material you have seen.
  • I am aiming to have each quiz take about 20 minutes or so to complete.
  • You must speak to me beforehand if you wish to be excused from a quiz.
  • Bring a calculator!
