The F&M-TGU Summer Program is an annual study-abroad opportunity jointly arranged by Franklin & Marshall and Tohoku Gakuin University in Sendai, Japan.

The five-week program is part of TGU's Japanese Studies Summer Program (JSSP) for foreign students.
