Welcome
    Welcome to the Fall 2008 edition of Astronomy 120: Introduction to Stars and Galaxies (Section A) taught by Froney Crawford. This web page will serve as the source of information, announcements, and downloads for this course. Please check it regularly.

    The required textbook for the course is The Cosmic Perspective (5th edition) by Bennett et al. The associated web site for the text (you can register here) is www.masteringastronomy.com.

    Labs for the course will be taught by Beth Praton (Tue nights), Froney Crawford (Wed nights), and Andrea Lommen (Thu nights). Details are in the lab manual and will be discussed in class and lab.

    Course announcements are located at the bottom of the page.
