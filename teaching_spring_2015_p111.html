Welcome
    Welcome to the Spring 2015 edition of Physics 111: Fundamental Physics I (Section A), taught by Froney Crawford. Labs for the course are being taught by a variety of other instructors. This web page will serve as the source of information, announcements, and downloads for this course.

    The textbook for the course is Physics for Scientists and Engineers (4th edition) by Giancoli. Note that we will use only Vol. 1 of this book in this course.
