Welcome
    Welcome to the Spring 2015 edition of Astronomy 121: Introduction to Astrophysics. Both the lab and the lecture part of the source will be taught by Froney Crawford. This web page will serve as the source of information, announcements, and downloads for this course (including the lab). Please check it regularly.

    The required textbook for the course is Quantitative Astronomy by Thomas L. Swihart.

    Some additional lab information will be handed out and discussed in person.

    Course announcements are located at the bottom of the page.
