Welcome

    This course is a one-credit Franklin and Marshall NSP course being taught at the Arcadia University's London Center by Froney Crawford. The dates for the course in London are July 4 through July 30, 2022. Most of the logistics involving travel, housing, etc. will be handled by the study abroad office in the Joseph International Center at F&M.

    Arcadia London has set up a canvas page for health and safety, covid, residence life and living in the UK (not specifically course related).

    The course syllabus and schedule is available on the course canvas page and has a lot of the academic details, including course information, a schedule of course meetings, and assignments.

    The required textbook for the course is Energy, Environment, and Climate (3nd edition) by Richard Wolfson.
