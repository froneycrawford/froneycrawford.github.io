Important Notes
  • Do the pre-lab questions for each lab and turn them in to Froney at the start of lab.
  • Lab reports are due to Froney (or under Froney's door, Koshland INSC Link L106) one week (by 4 pm) after the lab.
  • Formal lab report is due two weeks (by 4 pm) after the lab.
  • Labs which are up to a week late will be penalized 20%, more than a week late will be penalized 40%.
  • Important: All labs must be completed in a satisfactory manner to pass the course!
  • The assigned lab sections are available for Physics 101 and Physics 105.
  • If you are unsure of your writing skills for the formal report, try the Haverford writing center, which can be a useful resource.
