Welcome
    Welcome to the Fall 2010 edition of Astronomy 170: Survey of Astronomy (Section A) taught by Froney Crawford. This web page will serve as the source of information, announcements, and downloads for this course. Please check it regularly.

    The required textbook for the course is The Cosmic Perspective (6th edition) by Bennett et al. The associated web site for the text (you can register here) is www.masteringastronomy.com.

    Labs for the course will be taught by Beth Praton on Wed nights. Details are in the lab manual and will be discussed in class and lab.
