Crawford Invitational Tennis Tournament
Saturday May 31, 2003

Announcements:

  • The winner of the 2003 CITTS singles bracket is Dan Greenwood
  • The winners of the 2003 CITTS doubles bracket are Dan Greenwood / Lisa Nedorost

Some pictures are at the bottom of the page.

After a 20 year hiatus, we are pleased to announce the resumption of the Crawford Invitational Tennis Tournament, which will occur this year on Saturday May 31, 2003 (the Saturday after Memorial Day weekend).

The tournament is sponsored jointly by Dan Greenwood and Froney Crawford and will take place at Dan's house. We will have access to at least one tennis court at Dan's place and will have a party and barbecue during and after the tournament.

Details:

  • When: Saturday, May 31, 2003 starting at 2 pm for tennis and festivities.

  • Where: Chez Greenwood

  • What: Tennis tournament (during the day) and party (during the day and night). You can sleep over afterwards if you like.

  • RSVP: Please RSVP to Dan (dgreenwood@compexpress.com) or Froney (fcrawfor@haverford.edu) with your intentions so we can plan for the tournament bracket and buy enough food and drink.
